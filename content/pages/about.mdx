## About Me

A seasoned marketing and technology leader with over 20 years of experience in hiring and leading cross-functional teams and implementing data-driven strategies. As the current CMO at Elevate Aviation Group in Savannah, GA, I have a proven track record of elevating brand presence and driving significant growth, evidenced by my roles at a SaaS start-up, ten years of business ownership, and over 20 years of marketing experience including 10 years at Gulfstream Aerospace. I hold an MBA, am a proud US Army veteran, and am proficient in a range of technical tools and software development.
